Rules

  • Each turn the player rolls two dice.
  • If a player throws a 1 on any of the dice, he earns nothing and loses their turn.
  • Else the sum is added to the player's round score, until the player decides to stash their round score.
  • If a player stashes their score, the turn goes to other player.
  • If a player throws a 6 on any dice two times in a row, the player loses their turn and all points (round and total).
  • The first player to reach 100 or more points, wins the game.
